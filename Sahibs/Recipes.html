Gin and Tonic

Gin and tonic includes the following ingredients:
  1. 45ml Gin of choice
  2. 90ml Tonic water
  3. Ice cubes
Preparation
  1. Get a collins glass and fill it with icecubes
  2. Pour Gin into the glass
  3. Fill up the glass with tonic water
  4. Stir together and garnish with a lime wedge
